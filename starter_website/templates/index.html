browse_actor

under actor section, you can view the current_actor in the database, you can also add, update and delete a actor.

browse_director

under director section, you can view the current_director in the database, you can also add, update and delete a director.

browse_movie

under movie section, you can view the current_movie in the database, you can also add, update and delete a movie.(1:M relationship with director table) the movie can have 0 or 1 director but director can direct 0 or many movies.

browse_movie_cast

under movie_cast section, you can search for the movies that the specific actor has casted before , or you can search for the actors in the specific movie(m:m releationsip implementation)
